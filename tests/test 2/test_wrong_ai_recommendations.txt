Subject: SLAP AI chat giving completely wrong product recommendations

Hi,

Found a significant issue with the AI recommendation engine in SLAP.
When users ask for a product in a specific price range, the AI is
consistently recommending products outside that range — sometimes 3-4x
the stated budget.

Example: I asked "suggest a bluetooth speaker under ₹1500" and the AI
recommended JBL Charge 5 (₹7,499) and Sony SRS-XB23 (₹4,990). Neither
is under ₹1500.

This is happening across all product categories I tested —
electronics, footwear, and clothing.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP and start a new chat
2. Ask for a product recommendation with a price constraint
   (e.g. "show me running shoes under ₹2000")
3. Observe the recommendations returned by the AI
4. Check the actual prices of recommended items

Expected: All recommendations should be within the stated budget.
Actual: Recommendations frequently exceed the stated price constraint,
        sometimes by 2-5x.

Reproducibility: ~80% of the time. Occasionally it gets it right.

Impact:
Users are losing trust in the AI assistant. If users can't rely on
price filters, the core value proposition of SLAP breaks down.
Multiple user complaints coming in via support tickets.
Affects all platforms (Android + iOS + Web).

Environment:
- Platform: Android, iOS, Web (all affected)
- App Version: 2.4.1
- Tested across 3 accounts

Additional context: This might be a backend/model issue rather than
a frontend bug. The product catalog prices are correct in the regular
Flipkart app for the same items.

Screenshots attached.
