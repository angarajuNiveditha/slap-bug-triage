Subject: Login failing with "Failed to verify" error for some users

Hi Team,

Some users are unable to log into SLAP. They enter their phone number
and OTP correctly but get a "Failed to verify" error and cannot
proceed. The login just fails with no helpful message.

I reproduced this with the phone number 7087935097 — it consistently
fails to authenticate even with the correct OTP.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP app
2. Enter phone number 7087935097 on the login screen
3. Request and enter the correct OTP
4. Tap verify/submit
5. App shows "Failed to verify" error

Expected: User is authenticated and lands on the home screen.
Actual: Authentication fails with "Failed to verify" — user is
        stuck on the login screen and cannot access the app.

Reproducibility: 100% for this specific credential. May affect
                 other users too — need to investigate the scope.

Impact:
Affected users are completely locked out of SLAP. Cannot browse,
chat, or purchase anything. If this affects a broader set of
credentials it could be a significant login outage.

Environment:
- Platform: Android (v2.4.1), likely cross-platform
- Affected credential: 7087935097
- Auth flow: OTP based login
