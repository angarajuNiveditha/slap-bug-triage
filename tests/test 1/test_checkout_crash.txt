Subject: [URGENT] SLAP app crashes on checkout — all users affected, v2.4.2

Hi Team,

CRITICAL issue found during regression testing. The SLAP app crashes
to home screen the moment the user taps "Proceed to Pay" at the
checkout screen. This is 100% reproducible and affects ALL users on
the latest release v2.4.2.

This was working fine in v2.4.1. Something in the v2.4.2 release
broke the checkout flow entirely.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP on Android (v2.4.2)
2. Add any item to cart via AI chat or browse
3. Tap the cart icon
4. Review cart and tap "Proceed to Pay"
5. App immediately crashes to home screen

Expected: User is taken to the payment method selection screen.
Actual: App crashes silently — no error message, no crash dialog.

Reproducibility: 100% on Android v2.4.2. Tested on 5 devices.
iOS v2.4.2 NOT affected.

Impact:
Zero users can complete a purchase on Android v2.4.2.
This is a revenue-blocking P0. All Android orders are lost.
Estimated ~60% of our user base is on Android.

Environment:
- Platform: Android
- App version: 2.4.2 (build 20260608)
- OS: Android 12, 13, 14 (all affected)
- Devices tested: Pixel 6, Pixel 7, Samsung S21, Samsung S23, Redmi Note 12

Crash logs and screen recording attached: crash_checkout_v2.4.2.zip
