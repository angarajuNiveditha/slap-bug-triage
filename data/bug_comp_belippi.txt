Subject: Chat AI ignoring price filter — recommends products 3x over stated budget consistently

Hi,

Critical issue with the SLAP chat AI: when users state a budget in their query,
the backend recommendation engine is completely ignoring the price constraint.
Reproducing consistently across all product categories.

Example session:
  User: "Suggest running shoes under ₹2000"
  SLAP AI: Recommends Nike Air Zoom (₹5,499), Adidas Ultraboost (₹8,999)

The price filter is not being passed through the orchestration layer to the search
API. This is a backend issue — the AI parses the constraint correctly (confirmed
in logs) but the downstream product search call drops the price parameter.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP and start a new chat session
2. Enter: "suggest a bluetooth speaker under ₹1500"
3. Observe AI product recommendations
4. Check prices of recommended items against the stated budget

Expected: All AI recommendations should be within the stated price budget.
Actual: Recommendations consistently exceed the stated budget by 2-5x.
        Price constraint is silently dropped in the search orchestration.

Reproducibility: 100% — affects all chat sessions with price constraints.

Impact:
This breaks the fundamental promise of SLAP as a budget-aware shopping AI.
Users lose trust immediately when the AI ignores their price constraints.
Estimated 40% of SLAP chat sessions include a price filter query — all are
currently returning wrong results. Major revenue and retention impact.

Environment:
- Platform: Android, iOS, Web (all affected — backend issue)
- App Version: 2.4.1
- Root cause: price parameter dropped in orchestration layer → search API call
