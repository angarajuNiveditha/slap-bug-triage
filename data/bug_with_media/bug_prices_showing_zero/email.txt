Subject: 15 Minutes — product prices showing ₹0 for Muesli items (Cornflakes fine on same screen)

Hi Team,

In the SLAP "15 Minutes" (quick-commerce) flow, several product cards
are displaying their price as **₹0** instead of the actual price. This
is inconsistent — products in other categories on the SAME screen show
their prices correctly with discounts applied, so it's not a full
price-service outage. It's category- or product-specific.

Attached: screenshot1.png — taken from the 15 Minutes category-browse
view at address "D No: 3-548, 2nd Floor, Jawahar Vidya …".

What I see (top to bottom):

Cornflakes section — prices correct:
- Kellogg's Corn Flakes (53 g): -5%, ₹20 (strikethrough) ₹19 ✓
- Delish by Flipkart Corn Flakes (400 g): -57%, ₹150 (strikethrough) ₹65 ✓

Muesli section — prices BROKEN (showing ₹0):
- Bagrry's 16 in 1 Fruit & Nut Muesli (750 g, 4.3★) → **₹0** ✗
- Kellogg's Muesli Fruit & Seeds (500 g, 4.5★) → **₹0** ✗

(Fruit Jam section is collapsed, Peanut Butter section is partially
visible at the bottom — I did not scroll further to check those.)

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP, ensure address is selected (mine: Jawahar Vidya …)
2. Enter the "15 Minutes" quick-commerce flow (chat or category nav)
3. Land on the category-browse view with Cornflakes / Muesli / Fruit
   Jam / Peanut Butter sections
4. Observe: Cornflakes items show real prices and discount %,
   Muesli items show ₹0 with no strike-through and no discount

Expected:
Every product card shows a numeric price (and discount % where
applicable), the same way the Cornflakes items render.

Actual:
Muesli products show ₹0 — no MRP, no discount, no struck-through
price. The image, title, weight badge, star-rating and "+" Add button
are all rendered normally; only the price field is wrong.

Reproducibility:
3/3 cold-starts of the app, same address, same products. The Muesli
items consistently render ₹0 every time.

Concerns about downstream impact:
- If a user taps "+" on a ₹0 product, does the cart accept it at ₹0?
  This would let customers add items for free.
- If the price service is returning 0 vs the UI is rendering 0 incorrectly,
  the failure mode for cart/checkout is different — needs verification.
- Could mislead users into thinking the items are free / on sale.

Environment:
- Platform: iOS (iPhone-style status bar)
- App: SLAP, "15 Minutes" quick-commerce flow
- Time of report: 3:42 PM
- Network: Wi-Fi connected, cellular weak; other categories on the
  same screen are loading prices fine, so it's not a generic network
  issue.
- Address: D No: 3-548, 2nd Floor, Jawahar Vidya …

Impact:
Affects a subset of products / categories in the quick-commerce flow.
Could be a price-service / pricing-pipeline regression for specific
brand-category combinations, OR a UI rendering bug where the
price field defaults to 0 when some upstream field is null/missing.
Either way it erodes trust ("are these items really free?") and
potentially exposes us to ₹0 adds-to-cart if not handled defensively
downstream.
