Subject: [Feed/UI]: Discovery feed renders skeletons that never resolve — product images and chat input area stay as gray placeholders; taps on the cards do nothing

Hi Team,

The SLAP Discovery / Inspiration feed (the topic-card layout under
"Back-pain relief", "New parent", "Your best shades, styled for you",
etc.) is rendering its layout skeleton but most of the actual content
never fills in. Product card images stay as gray rectangles for the
entire ~20-second recording — only a couple of product images appear
near the bottom of the feed. The chat-input / suggestions area at the
top also renders as empty gray rectangles. Tapping the "Ask SLAP"
chips on the placeholder cards does nothing — no navigation, no
chat opens, no error toast.

Attached: bug_tap_unresponsive.mp4 — screen recording on Android
that walks through the feed. I'm scrolling slowly so you can see the
gray placeholder cards persisting under three different topic
sections, plus the broken header area.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP on Android with a logged-in account
2. Navigate to the Discovery / Inspiration feed (the feed of
   curated topic cards — "Back-pain relief: Professional WFH",
   "New parent: Sleep-safe nursery", "Your best shades, styled for
   you", "Bamboo vs Cotton for bedsheets", etc.)
3. Wait 15+ seconds for content to load
4. Observe: under every topic card, the two product-card slots show
   gray placeholder rectangles where the product images should
   be. Some cards have a small loading spinner inside that never
   resolves. Titles and short descriptions DO render below the
   placeholder ("Lumbar support cushions", "Adjustable laptop stands",
   "Baby monitors with WiFi", "Silent HEPA purifiers"), but the
   product images don't.
5. Tap the "Ask SLAP" chip on any of the placeholder product cards
   → nothing happens; no chat opens, no navigation, no error.
6. Scroll to the bottom of the feed → eventually a few cards under
   "Your best shades, styled for you" DO show real product images
   (men's Nehru jackets, shirts — "maksud enterprise Sleev...",
   "Vastraa Fusion Sleevele...", "Badoliya"), but everything above
   is still placeholders.
7. The chat input region at the top of the screen also shows empty
   gray rectangles instead of the normal chat entry field with
   suggestion chips.

Expected:
- Product card images in the Discovery feed render within 1–2
  seconds of the card becoming visible
- Tapping "Ask SLAP" on a product card opens the chat with that
  product's query pre-filled (or routes to the relevant PDP)
- The chat input field at the top is fully rendered and tappable

Actual:
- Most product card images never load — stay as gray placeholders
- "Ask SLAP" chip taps on placeholder cards are silently swallowed
  (no navigation, no error). Cards are inert.
- The chat input / suggestion-chip area at the top is also stuck
  as skeletal placeholders
- Only the last topic card in the recording ("Your best shades")
  partially recovers — three real product cards visible at the
  bottom of the frame, but cards above remain broken

Reproducibility:
Consistent across the full ~20-second recording. The feed never
recovered to a fully-loaded state while I was looking at it. I have
not tried a full app restart yet — flagging quickly because the
"silent tap" behaviour is the worst kind of bug to ship (user thinks
the UI is just slow, taps repeatedly, gives up).

Impact:
- Discovery feed is a primary entry point for browsing intent — if
  product images don't load and the chips don't respond, the user
  can't engage with curated topics.
- The "silent tap" failure mode (tap does nothing, no error) is
  particularly bad — users will conclude the app is broken rather
  than report a specific error.
- Direct chat ("Ask SLAP" at the home / chat surface) seems unaffected
  based on what's visible, but the feed-driven discovery path is
  effectively dead in this state.

Could be:
- Image CDN / product-image-service failure for the discovery payload
- A discovery-API timeout where text fields hydrate but the image
  URLs / card-action handlers come from a separate hydration call
  that's failing
- A React Native render race where placeholders are mounted but
  never replaced because the data callback never fires
- Touch-handler regression on the placeholder cards (handlers wired
  to the loaded state, so they're no-ops while in skeleton state)

Environment:
- Platform: Android (status bar shows VoLTE + Wi-Fi + 5G/LTE bars;
  clock 11:48 IST)
- App: SLAP, latest build I have installed
- Device: Android phone (Pixel-style chrome)
- Network: Wi-Fi + cellular both showing strong signal; the partial
  image recovery near the bottom of the feed suggests not a hard
  network outage — image requests are succeeding for SOME cards
  but not most.
