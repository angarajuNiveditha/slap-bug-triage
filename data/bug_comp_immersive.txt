Subject: [Native AR] VTO SDK crashing with ANR on Android 14 — native AR not responding

Hi Team,

The native AR module in SLAP is producing Application Not Responding (ANR) errors
on Android 14 devices. When a user opens the Virtual Try-On (VTO) feature via the
AR try-on button on the product page, the native AR layer freezes and the OS throws
an ANR dialog within 5 seconds.

This is happening in the VTO SDK integration layer (drishyamukh-core), not in the
React Native bridge. The AR session initialises but hangs before rendering the first
frame. Tested with the latest AR library version.

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open SLAP on Android 14 (Pixel 8 or Samsung S24)
2. Navigate to any fashion product page
3. Tap the "Try it on" AR button
4. Wait — native AR session starts to initialise
5. App freezes, ANR dialog appears within ~5 seconds

Expected: AR camera opens, virtual try-on overlay renders on user's image.
Actual: Native AR crashes with ANR. drishyamukh-core throws a fatal exception.
        No graceful fallback — user is stuck on the ANR dialog.

Reproducibility: 100% on Android 14. Android 13 is unaffected.

Impact:
Virtual Try-On is a key differentiator for SLAP's fashion vertical. ANR on
Android 14 (which represents ~30% of our Android base) completely blocks the
feature for those users. Also impacts Play Store rating due to ANR reports.

Environment:
- Platform: Android (Android 14 only)
- App Version: 2.4.2
- Component: native AR / VTO SDK / drishyamukh-core
- Devices tested: Pixel 8, Pixel 8 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S24
